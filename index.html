Meet the maker

Hi, I'm Patricia.

I've been dyeing fabric for over a decade — first as a way to make my kids' baby-wearing wraps feel like ours, and then because people kept asking where I got them. Today every piece in this shop is dyed, washed, photographed and packed by me, in small batches, in my home studio.

I work in ice-dye because I love that I can never quite predict the result. The colors find their own path. That's the part I want you to wear.

— Patricia
